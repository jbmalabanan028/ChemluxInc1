NSF International Certifies Chemlux Incorporated

NSF International Certifies Chemlux Incorporated Chemlux Incorporated is now certified and recognized by NSF International as a complying company with the NSF registration guidelines for proprietary substances and non food compounds. NSF International is the only international independent, third party organization that offers product registration for nonfood compounds used in food and beverage processing. It was established to evaluate the risk of contamination of chemical compounds used in and around food processing facility. With this certification, our clients are assured that Chemlux Incorporated products listed in the NSF White Book(www.nsfwhitebook.org) is compliant with food safety regulations and has passed NSF's thorough toxicological assessment.
